About GK Foods

GK Foods started as a small home kitchen with a passion for authentic, homemade food. Our founder, Geetha Krishnan, began by preparing traditional batters and curries for family and friends, who quickly fell in love with the taste and quality.

Today, we continue to use the same traditional recipes, prepared with love and the finest ingredients, without any preservatives or artificial additives.
